House Keeping Services

Busy schedules make it difficult to find time to maintain a suitable home. The best way to ensure that your home is always clean and tidy is to hire our team at Excellence Maids because we recognize that a clean house is a happy home, which is why we are proud to offer comprehensive home cleaning services. We also realize that every family has their preferences, so we accommodate all their specific requests in their cleaning plan. Therefore, whenever you want to update your cleaning plan, just contact our team. We encourage you to give us as many instructions as you want so that our housekeepers can build a plan tailored to your home needs, and preferences.

Here's what our cleaning services include:

  • Vacuum - We vacuum every floor in each room. This includes stairs, carpet rooms, wood, linoleum, and tiled floors. Let's remove mobile pads and vacuum crumbs, pet hair, and dust.
  • Dust – We dust every surface of your home to reduce allergens. This includes robes, moldings, luminaires, fan blades, window sills, shutters, wardrobes, entertainment centers, dressers, side tables, and more. We will remove cobwebs in the corners of the ceiling and make sure that no dust rabbits are left behind.
  • Mop - We scrub the floors of wood, tile, and linoleum. We're also going to clean the dirty baseboards.
  • Hygiene – We sanitize high-use areas such as bathrooms, kitchen countertops, and doorknobs.
  • Clean – We clean and scrub bathroom faucets, sink basins, mirrors, vanities, countertops, and surroundings. We scrub soap and tile slag, grouts, and glass shower doors. In the kitchen, we clean the top of the stove, control buttons, cabinets, countertops, and splashes on the back. We clean the grease and food splashes in the microwave and clean the outside of all appliances.
  • Finishing touches – We empty all trash cans, fluffy pillows, and straighten chairs and area rugs.
