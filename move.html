Move In & Out Cleaning

Now that the rooms are empty (or almost empty), it's time to make your house or apartment look even better than when you first moved in. Many apartments and home contracts require "broom cleaning", while others ask for a detailed checklist that covers everything from the stove to the baseboards.

The services included in our standard change cleaning checklist can include:

  • Vacuum and sweep all floors
  • Clean the baseboards
  • Mop and bathtub
  • Scrub bathrooms
  • Clean sinks and countertops
  • Clean kitchen appliances
  • Dust all

    • We will take care of the cleaning so you can focus on your move. If you need help on the other side also Excellence Maids is here to help! Performing a thorough cleaning before moving is beneficial to both parties, especially if you have a security deposit on the line. However, the move in cleaning is easy to overlook. After all, you have bigger things to worry about, like moving into your new home or apartment. Excellence Maids offers flexible cleaning and moving services to facilitate the transition to your new space. Whether you need the whole house cleaned or just certain items on your checklist, our professional cleaners are ready to help.
