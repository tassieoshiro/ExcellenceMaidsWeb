Recurring Cleaning

Excellence Maids recognize how life can be occupied. That's why we design cleaning service packages based on your specific preferences. It is our job to make your life easier, and we strive to make it as worry-free as possible.

While we can customize your cleaning plan to suit your needs, most customers schedule regular cleaning services:

  • Once a week - This is a popular solution for busy homes.
  • Every two weeks - This service works best to help with general maintence.
  • Once a month - We will do a thorough cleaning of your home when you need it.

    • Most of our customers prefer routine visits from our professional janitors. Depending on the activity your home sees, our weekly and fortnightly services may be the best solution for you. They will give you more free time on your busy schedule.
